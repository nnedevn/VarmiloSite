Features

If these keyboards look awfully familiar, it’s because they were constructed in the same factory as the ever-popular Leopolds. Featuring nearly identical construction to the FC660M while adding in a navigation cluster and optional backlighting, the Varmilo Mechanical Keyboard lets you get close to the King without paying too hefty a tax.

Depending on what kind of feel and durability you’re looking for in your legends, we have just your type. Laser-engraved legends are burned right into the keycap and the deep grooves are covered with infill to retain a smooth feel. For long-lasting construction with no surface imbalance, dye sublimated legends infuse the plastic with dye, the most durable possible construction that also retains a smooth hand feel.

Underneath the keycaps, Cherry MX switches give you enough get-up-and-go to last 50 million actuations, providing a light tactile feedback with each stroke. The keyboard connects to your computer via a USB A to mini-B cable, stretching to almost seven feet for a far larger reach than most. For a simply sophisticated typing experience without a single distraction, get clacking today on the Varmilo Mechanical Keyboard.
